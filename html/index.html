About Me

My name is Mason, I'm a life-time techie and gamer. The first game I remember playing is Armor Alley on the PC, and I've been hooked ever since. Besides gaming, I love being outdoors, I hike and backpack when time allows and the call of adventure beckons beyond the console. I am married to a wonderful woman named Melissa, and we have an Australian Shepherd name Panda(she's a dog shepherd, not a human shepard from Australia) and two night-terrors mascarading as the common cat: London, and Pipsqueak. I am working towards becoming a full-stack web developer, and hopefully, by the time anyone is looking at this page in the hopes of recruiting me, I will be able to update this to say with confidence that I have accomplished that.
